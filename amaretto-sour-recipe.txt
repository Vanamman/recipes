Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 oz Amaretto Almond Liquor
- 1-2 splashes of Sweet and Sour mix

Directions:
1. Pour the drink into a cocktail shaker half-filled with ice cubes

2. Add a splash or two of Sweet and Sour mix, and shake well

3. Strain or pour into an old-fashioned glass

4. Garnish with a maraschino cherry and a slice of orange

5. Serve
